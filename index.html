About WinningWriter

WinningWriter is a pattern-extraction tool built from real accepted and best-paper submissions at academic conferences. Rather than generic writing advice, every pattern here is sourced directly from papers that reviewers actually accepted.

The database grows with each conference. Currently covers IES (International Electronics Symposium, PENS Surabaya) with 4 analyzed papers — 2 best papers and 2 accepted papers.

Planned: IEEE TENCON, ICICI-BME, EECCIS, and more. If you have accepted papers you'd like to contribute for analysis, the patterns will be extracted and added here.

Each conference page includes a full paper breakdown, figure strategy guide, DO/DON'T patterns, and an interactive pre-submission checklist sourced from the analyzed papers.
